About Us - Cervival of the Fittest

Cervival Inc. is a manufacture and marketer of extreme sports equipments under its " Cervival " brand. The company was founded in 2006 by a group of sports enthusiast sharing a common vision; " ......"

Goals

Our primary focus is providing high quality boards, wetsuits, apparels, accessories and related products for adventurous, outdoor, and thrill-seeking people of all ages.

Unique Handmade Boards, Competitive Price

Cervival's biggest strength lies within our ability to design and manufacture our entire range of product lines. For that very same reason, we are able to offer lower price without sacrificing quality. All boards are hand-made by our highly trained shapers, and apparel lines are produced with highest quality of fabrics. Due to our unique business model, we are able to produce in your pattern design in small quantity.

Faith

" Cervival of the Fittest " is the backbone of the company and the spirit within the brand. We as humans have come to Evolve, Adapt and Survive the most extreme conditions that the Mother Nature has ever brought upon us, but at the same time, it has provided us with playgrounds for many revolutionized sports. We believe that all humans are genetically built for seeking adventure and challenge.
