Star Sighting Title

2010/02/15

Are you sick of riding the same machine shaped surfboard as the guy next to you in the line-up? Do you want to become a complete surfer? If yes, then there is no better way then to make your own surfboard. Normally you enter a shop, buy a board and spend endless hours in the ocean. If the board doesn't work you sell it and get a new one. But you'll never know why it didn't work: Was it the rails, the concave or tail shape? This is about to change! You will make your own surfboard and begin to understand how complex a surfboard really is.

Riley Balsawood Surfboards has specialised in shaping kits and instructions which make it a breeze to make a surfboard even for complete novices. In the new secure online shop products range from instructional Ebooks, DVDs, fin blanks all the way to complete shaping kits (solid and with a recycled EPS core) starting at just $280 AUD - 170 EURO - 245 USD. The new shop offers products for hard core- and recreational shapers alike. All items are shipped worldwide at very competitive prices.
